Welcome

cn.bbs.* newsgroups started life on Oct 3, 2001. Ask your ISP to add the newsgroups for you. There are currently more than 110 cn.bbs.* newsgroups. cn.bbs.* newsgroups are for worldwide distribution. We hope everyone feels welcome.

Overview

The cn.bbs.* Newsgroups Administration is a volunteer effort to manage and develop the cn.bbs.* USENET news hierarchy as a worldwide resource. cn.bbs.* is well-managed according to best-practices. For general chat and discussion, use the newsgroup cn.bbs.admin. If you are new to Usenet and/or cn.bbs.* post your questions to cn.bbs.admin. Discussion on the policy for cn.bbs.* should take place on cn.bbs.admin newsgroup. Please read the relevant FAQs on cn.bbs.* policy before sending any questions. This web site should cover most questions you may have.

Current Policy

  • No binaries policy.
  • Aim to form Administrative Group to co-ordinate volunteers.
  • Encourage news admins to respond to checkgroups messages.
